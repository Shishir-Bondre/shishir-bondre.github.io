About Us

What we do?

We provide SEBI-compliant automated trading strategies. Whether you’re new to investing or a seasoned trader, our algorithms handle everything: spotting opportunities, managing risks, and closing trades on time.

Why choose us?

  • Zero Hassle Setup: Don’t have a demat account? We’ll help you create one instantly with our SEBI-registered partner broker.
  • No Fund Handling: Your money stays in your account. We only execute trades, never touch your funds.
  • Total Control: Start or stop the algorithm anytime. Your portfolio, your rules.

How It Works?

Subscribe and Set up payment

Choose a plan that fits your goals. Link your UPI for hassle-free fee auto-debit.

Create/Link Demat Account

New to trading? We’ll create a demat account for you. Already have one? Link it securely in minutes.

Sign POA (Power of Attorney)

E-sign a quick, secure POA to authorize trade execution. No fund access—we only place trades.

Activate Algorithm

“Run Algorithm.” Our tech takes over, 24/7

Relax & Monitor

Trades execute automatically. Track performance anytime.

Settlement & Closure

Auto-Close Trades: All positions close at contract end. Fee Settlement: Fees debited via UPI mandate. Secure Revoke: API access ends; your account stays yours.
